§04 · Approach
Code is structure. Design is empathy. The best products need both, so I refuse to choose.
As an engineer

I think in systems, edge cases, and architecture that holds up under load, and I lead teams through software design, reviews, and the unglamorous work of shipping reliably.

As a designer

I start from the person on the other side of the screen, then sweat the type, the spacing, and the flow until the interface disappears and the task is all that's left.
